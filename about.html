About The Florida Hotel

Our Story

Welcome to The Florida Hotel, where luxury meets comfort in the heart of Florida. Established in 1995, our hotel has been serving guests with exceptional hospitality and world-class amenities for over 25 years. Our commitment to excellence and attention to detail has made us a preferred destination for both business and leisure travelers.

Located in a prime area with easy access to major attractions, shopping districts, and business centers, The Florida Hotel offers the perfect blend of convenience and tranquility.

Our Mission
